## Executive Summary

**Total annual costs range widely**, from SGD 8,000 (Malaysia public) to SGD 110,000 (Australia Go8 Medicine). Most Singaporean students choose destinations balancing cost, quality, and post-study work pathways.

### The Big Picture

| Tier | Total Annual Cost (SGD) | Destinations |
|------|------------------------|-------------|
| **Budget** | $8,000 – $17,000 | Malaysia (public), Germany (tuition-free), Taiwan |
| **Mid-Range** | $15,000 – $30,000 | Malaysia (branch campuses), China, Japan (public), Netherlands |
| **Premium** | $30,000 – $55,000 | Hong Kong, Ireland, Canada (regional), Japan (private), UK (non-London) |
| **Luxury** | $55,000 – $110,000 | UK (London), Australia (Go8), USA, Canada (Toronto/Vancouver) |

**Top value destinations for Singaporeans:**
1. **Germany** — tuition-free, high quality, 18-month post-study visa
2. **Malaysia** — SGD 8-17k/yr, 30-min drive, same food/culture
3. **Japan (public)** — SGD 13-21k/yr, MEXT scholarships available, strong tech industry
4. **Taiwan** — SGD 10-15k/yr, Mandarin advantage, generous MOE scholarships

### Work Rights
- 120 full days/year part-time work permitted
- Post-study **18-month job search visa**
- PR possible after 33 months (Blue Card) or 2 years (after certain conditions)

## 16. Financing Your Overseas Education

### Education Loans
| Source | Product | Loan Amount | Interest | Notes |
|--------|---------|------------|----------|-------|
| **OCBC** | Education Loan | Up to $160,000 (for specific countries) | 4.88% p.a. fixed | For UG studies abroad; up to 10 years repayment |
| **DBS** | EduLoan | Up to $200,000 | 4.5-5.5% p.a. | For specific countries; flexible repayment |
| **CPF** | CPF Education Scheme | Cannot use CPF for overseas UG | N/A | CPF only for local UG studies |

### Key Financial Tips
1. **Total cost = tuition + living + airfare + insurance + visa fees.** Budget an extra 10-15% as buffer.
2. **Exchange rate risk:** SGD has been strong against GBP/EUR (good for UK/Europe). Monitor rates and consider forward contracts or multi-currency accounts.
3. **Work while studying:** Use the permitted 20 hrs/week to offset living costs. At UK min wage (~£11.44/hr), 20 hrs = ~£915/month (≈SGD 1,570) — covers most of your non-tuition costs.
4. **Apply early for scholarships** (October-January of final JC/Poly year).
5. **Consider regional campuses:** Monash Malaysia → transfer to Australia saves 1-2 years of Australian tuition.
6. **Watch the VAT/GST:** Ireland's 23% VAT, UK's 20% — these add to daily costs.
