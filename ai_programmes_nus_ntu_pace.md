## 1. NUS Programmes

### 1.1 NUS SCALE / NUS L3

**NUS SCALE** (School of Continuing and Lifelong Education) is the university's central PACE unit, coordinating lifelong learning across all NUS faculties. SCALE offers:

- **SkillsFuture Series courses** funded by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) — short, industry-relevant courses in AI, Data, Cybersecurity, Sustainability, and more.
- **Stackable credential pathway:** Short Course → Graduate Certificate → (Graduate Diploma) → Master's degree.
- **SCTP (SkillsFuture Career Transition Programme)** — full course fee subsidies and training allowances for mid-career career switchers.
- **Executive Education & Digital Learning** partnerships (e.g., Coursera for Business, Emeritus).

Key programmes under SCALE include the NUS MComp suite (administered by School of Computing) and the NUS-ISS stackable pathway.

**Website:** scale.nus.edu.sg

### 1.2 NUS Master of Computing in Artificial Intelligence (Part-time)

**Previously:** Master of Computing (Artificial Intelligence Specialisation)
**From AY2026/2027:** New dedicated **Master of Computing in Artificial Intelligence (MComp in AI)** programme.

| Aspect | Details |
|---|---|
| **Offered by** | NUS School of Computing (SoC) |
| **Programme type** | Coursework master's (with dissertation option) |
| **Total units** | 40 Units (Modular Credits) |
| **Part-time duration** | 2–4 years (max. candidature) |
| **Full-time duration** | 1.5–2 years (also available) |
| **Tuition fees (full)** | ~SGD 67,144 (incl. 9% GST, AY2026/2027 intake) |
| **Approx. Singaporean subsidised** | ~SGD 40,000–50,000 (after MOE subsidy) |
| **Intake** | January, August (semester system) |
| **Delivery** | Evening classes (most level 5000 courses), some online components |
| **SCTP eligible?** | Yes |
| **SkillsFuture MCES eligible?** | Yes (Singaporeans 40+) |

#### Programme Structure

- **Coursework option:** 40 Units = 5 core courses + 5 elective courses (or 8 courses + capstone project)
- **Dissertation option:** 40 Units = coursework + a dissertation (equivalent to 2–3 courses)
- Most courses are 4 Units each.

#### Core Courses (indicative)

| Code | Course |
|---|---|
| CS5242 | Neural Networks and Deep Learning |
| CS5246 | Text Mining |
| CS5248 | Interactive Media |
| CS5339 | Machine Learning |
| CS5446 | AI Planning |
| CS5340 | Probabilistic AI / Uncertainty Modelling |
| CS5344 | Big-Data Analytics Technology |

#### Elective Courses (AI specialisation area)

Courses drawn from the full MComp AI basket plus cross-listings from Computer Science, Information Systems, and Infocomm Security tracks. Typical offerings include reinforcement learning, computer vision, NLP, AI ethics, multi-agent systems, and AI in healthcare/finance.

#### Entry Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Computing or a related discipline (or equivalent work experience)
- Relevant work experience preferred for part-time applicants
- TOEFL/IELTS for non-English undergraduate instruction
- GRE not mandatory (but may strengthen application)

#### Notes

- The MComp in AI replaces the previous AI Specialisation within the generic MComp. From AY2026/2027, applicants apply directly to the MComp in AI programme.
- NUS Enhanced Tuition Fee Rebate may be available for Singapore Citizens and PRs.
- SG Digital Scholarship (Postgraduate) available for outstanding applicants.

**Website:** comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/pg/mcomp-ai/

### 1.3 NUS Graduate Certificates in AI / Data Science

NUS offers stackable **Graduate Certificates** that can ladder into the full MComp. Each Graduate Certificate is 16 Units (4 courses).

| Graduate Certificate | Focus Area | Fee (approx) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Graduate Certificate in Foundation of AI | Core AI fundamentals, ML, deep learning | SGD 8,000–12,000 | 6–12 months |
| Graduate Certificate in AI Applications | Applied NLP, computer vision, AI systems | SGD 8,000–12,000 | 6–12 months |

**Key points:**

- Graduate Certificates can be taken **standalone** (no commitment to full master's) or count towards the MComp degree.
- Stackable design: complete one or two Graduate Certificates, then apply credits toward the MComp.
- Evening classes, designed for working professionals.
- SkillsFuture Credit and MCES eligible (where approved).

### 1.4 NUS Executive Education & Short Courses in AI

#### NUS Business School

- **AI for Business** — Executive short course covering AI strategy, use cases, and organisational adoption. No coding required. Typically 2–5 days.
- **AI Strategy for Leaders** — For senior executives who need to lead AI transformation.
- **NUS Accelerated Management Programme (AMP)** — Includes AI modules.
- Fees: SGD 2,000–8,000 per programme.

- **Machine Learning with Python** — Hands-on programming course

- SkillsFuture Credit eligible — Singaporeans can use their $500 credit (and top-ups).
- Monthly/quarterly intakes.

#### NUS via Emeritus Partnership

- **NUS AI, ML and Data Science Programme** (via Emeritus) — Online, cohort-based, 6–9 months
- Certificate from NUS, designed for working professionals
- Fees: ~SGD 5,000–8,000

#### NUS on Coursera

- NUS partners with Coursera to offer select digital learning courses, including AI/ML topics.
- Flexible, self-paced, primarily foundational level.

### 1.5 NUS-ISS Stackable Programmes

The **NUS Institute of Systems Science (NUS-ISS)** offers an alternative, more applied pathway distinct from the School of Computing's MComp. This is the **Stackable Certificate Programme in Artificial Intelligence**.

#### Pathway Structure

```
Short Course (Certificate of Completion)
    → Graduate Certificate (4 courses)
        → Graduate Diploma (2 Graduate Certificates = 8 courses)
            → Master of Technology in Artificial Intelligence Systems (MTech AIS)
```

**Note:** The MTech AIS was formerly known as Master of Technology in Intelligent Systems.

#### Graduate Certificates (AI Track)

| Graduate Certificate | Focus |
|---|---|
| Graduate Certificate in Architecting AI Systems | Responsible AI, AI security, Agentic AI, MLSecOps, system design |
| Graduate Certificate in Intelligent Reasoning Systems | Knowledge representation, reasoning, expert systems |
| Graduate Certificate in Practical Language Processing | NLP, LLMs, text analytics |
| Graduate Certificate in Pattern Recognition Systems | Computer vision, deep learning for vision |

*(Participants choose from available GCs; new GCs are introduced periodically as the field evolves.)*

#### Fees & Duration

| Level | Duration | Fees (approx) |
|---|---|---|
| Graduate Certificate | 6–9 months (part-time) | SGD 8,000–12,000 per GC |
| Graduate Diploma | 2 GCs = 12–18 months | SGD 16,000–24,000 |
| Master of Technology (AIS) | ~2 years (part-time) | SGD 25,000–35,000 total |

#### Key differences from SoC MComp

- **More applied, less theoretical** — emphasis on building and deploying AI systems, not research.
- **Industry project component** — MTech includes a substantial capstone project applying AI to a real business problem.
- **Broader entry requirements** — open to non-computing degree holders with relevant IT experience.
- **SkillsFuture MCES eligible** — Singaporeans 40+ can get up to 90% subsidy on selected courses.
- **SCTP eligible** — Full subsidy available for eligible career switchers.

**Website:** iss.nus.edu.sg/stackable-certificate-programmes/intelligent-systems

## 2. NTU Programmes

### 2.1 NTU PaCE

**NTU PaCE** (Professional and Continuing Education) / **Academy for Professional and Continuing Education** is the central PACE unit for NTU, offering:

- **Stackable credential pathway:** Certificate of Completion → Graduate Certificate (6 AU) → FlexiMasters (15 AU) → Master's degree (30 AU)
- **Modular, credit-bearing courses** that stack toward formal qualifications
- **SkillsFuture Career Transition Programme (SCTP)** for mid-career professionals
- **Custom corporate training** via NTUitive

NTU PaCE administers the FlexiMasters framework — a unique intermediate qualification between Graduate Certificate and full Master's, providing a credential at 15 AU (half a master's).

**Website:** pace.ntu.edu.sg

## 3. Comparison: NUS vs NTU

| Aspect | NUS | NTU |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship AI Master's** | MComp in AI — 40 Units | MSAI — 30 AU |
| **Applied AI Master's** | MTech AIS via ISS (applied) | MCAAI via CCDS (applied, new) |
| **Part-time duration** | 2–4 years | 2–4 years |
| **Full fees (unsubsidised)** | ~SGD 67,144 (MComp AI) | ~SGD 56,000–63,000 (MSAI) / ~SGD 65,400 (MCAAI) |
| **Approx. subsidised (SC/PR)** | ~SGD 40,000–50,000 | ~SGD 30,000–45,000 |
| **Stackable pathway** | Cert → GradCert → GradDip → MComp / MTech | Cert → GradCert (6AU) → FlexiMasters (15AU) → MSc (30AU) |
| **PACE unit** | SCALE / NUS-ISS | PaCE@NTU |
| **Focus (SoC programme)** | Theoretical + applied (research-informed) | Balance of theory and practice |
| **Focus (ISS / MCAAI)** | Applied, industry-project based | Applied, responsible AI, cross-disciplinary |
| **SkillsFuture MCES** | Yes (Singaporeans 40+, up to 90%) | Yes (Singaporeans 40+, up to 90%) |
| **SCTP eligible** | Yes | Yes |
| **Intake** | January, August | August (primary), January (limited) |
| **Delivery mode** | Evening classes + some online | Evening classes, on-campus |
| **Entry requirements** | Computing degree + experience | Relevant degree + 2+ years experience preferred |
| **AI research reputation** | Very strong (SoC is top-10 globally) | Very strong (especially computer vision, NLP) |
| **QS AI/Data Science rank** | #4 globally (2025) | #5 globally (2025) |

### Key Differences Summary

1. **NUS offers two distinct AI master's pathways:**
   - **SoC MComp in AI** — more academic/research-oriented, harder entry, 40 Units
   - **ISS MTech AIS** — more applied, easier entry, stackable via Graduate Certificates

2. **NTU offers two parallel AI master's:**
   - **MSAI** — theory-heavy, requires CS/STEM background, 30 AU
   - **MCAAI (new)** — applied, open to non-CS, 30 AU, stackable via FlexiMasters

3. **NTU's FlexiMasters** (15 AU) is a unique intermediate credential — a half-master's that's more substantial than a Graduate Certificate but less than a full degree.

4. **Cost:** NUS MComp is more expensive at full fee (~SGD 67K) than NTU MSAI (~SGD 56-63K), but both are comparable after subsidies. NUS ISS MTech (~SGD 25-35K total) is the most affordable option.

## 4. Stackable Credential Pathways

### NUS Pathways

#### Pathway A: NUS SoC (Academic)
```
Short Course (non-credit)
  → Graduate Certificate in AI (4 courses = 16 MCs) [~SGD 10K, 6–12 months]
    → Second Graduate Certificate (4 courses = 16 MCs) [~SGD 10K, 6–12 months]
      → MComp in AI (final 8 MCs / capstone / dissertation) [~SGD 30–50K total]
```
**Total for full master's via stacking:** ~SGD 40,000–67,000
**Time:** 1.5–4 years depending on pace

#### Pathway B: NUS-ISS (Applied)
```
Short Course (Certificate of Completion)
  → Graduate Certificate in AI Systems (4 courses) [~SGD 10K, 6–9 months]
    → Graduate Diploma (2 GCs = 8 courses) [~SGD 20K, 12–18 months]
      → Master of Technology in AI Systems (GD + Capstone Project) [~SGD 25–35K total]
```
**Total for full master's via stacking:** ~SGD 25,000–35,000
**Time:** 1.5–2.5 years depending on pace

### NTU Pathways

#### Pathway C: NTU MSAI (Theoretical)
```
Short Course (non-credit)
  → Graduate Certificate in AI Fundamentals (6 AU) [~SGD 9K, 6 months]
    → FlexiMasters in AI Fundamentals (15 AU) [~SGD 22K, 12–18 months]
      → MSc in Artificial Intelligence — MSAI (30 AU) [~SGD 56–63K total]
```
**Total for full master's via stacking:** ~SGD 56,000–63,000
**Time:** 1.5–4 years depending on pace

#### Pathway D: NTU MCAAI (Applied)
```
Short Course (non-credit)
  → Graduate Certificate in Responsible Applied AI (6 AU) [~SGD 9K, 6 months]
    → FlexiMasters in Responsible Applied AI (15 AU) [~SGD 22K, 12–18 months]
      → Master of Computing in Applied AI — MCAAI (30 AU) [~SGD 65K total]
```
**Total for full master's via stacking:** ~SGD 65,000
**Time:** 1.5–4 years depending on pace

## 5. SkillsFuture & Financial Considerations

### SkillsFuture Credit (SFC)

| Item | Amount | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|
| Initial credit (2016) | SGD 500 | All Singaporeans aged 25+ |
| Top-up (2020) | SGD 500 | All Singaporeans aged 25+ |
| Mid-Career top-up (2024+) | SGD 4,000 | Singaporeans aged 40+ |
| **Total (if all received)** | **SGD 5,000** | |

- Can be used for approved courses on the SkillsFuture portal (courses.myskillsfuture.gov.sg).
- Many NUS SCALE, NUS-ISS, and NTU PaCE courses are SkillsFuture Credit eligible.

### SkillsFuture Mid-Career Enhanced Subsidy (MCES)

| Eligibility | Subsidy |
|---|---|
| Singaporeans aged 40+ | Up to **90%** of course fees |
| Singapore PRs aged 40+ | Up to **90%** of course fees (select programmes) |

- Applies to selected SSG-funded courses at NUS and NTU.
- Includes many Graduate Certificate and short course programmes.
- Does NOT typically apply to full master's degree programmes (these are MOE-subsidised instead).

### SkillsFuture Career Transition Programme (SCTP)

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **What it covers** | Full course fee subsidy for eligible career switchers |
| **Training allowance** | Up to SGD 3,000/month for full-time training |
| **Eligibility** | Singaporeans aged 21+, new industry/job role |
| **NUS SCTP programmes** | NUS SCALE and NUS Computing SCTP tracks |
| **NTU SCTP programmes** | NTU PaCE SCTP — e.g., Advanced Professional Certificate in Data Science and AI |
| **Duration** | 3–12 months (full-time or part-time) |

### MOE Subsidy (Master's Programmes)

| Status | Subsidised fee (approx) | Full fee |
|---|---|---|
| Singapore Citizen | ~40–60% of full fee | SGD 56K–67K (varies) |
| Singapore PR | ~30–50% of full fee | SGD 56K–67K (varies) |
| International Student | No subsidy (unless Tuition Grant) | SGD 56K–67K (varies) |

- Both NUS MComp and NTU MSAI/MCAAI are MOE-subsidised for Singaporeans/PRs.
- Tuition Grant Scheme (TGS) requires a 3-year work bond in Singapore for international students.

### Other Financial Schemes

| Scheme | Benefit | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|
| **NTU CCDS Subsidy** | SGD 5,000 lump sum (MCAAI) | All Singaporeans and PRs |
| **NTU Financial Aid** | Up to SGD 15,000 (MCAAI) | Singaporeans/PRs with financial need |
| **Union Training Assistance Programme (UTAP)** | 50% of unfunded course fees (capped SGD 250/yr) | NTUC members |
| **Company Sponsorship** | Full or partial fee coverage | Employer-dependent |
| **NUS Enhanced Tuition Fee Rebate** | Variable | Singapore Citizens and PRs (selected programmes) |
| **SG Digital Scholarship** | Full fees + allowance | Outstanding Singaporeans in tech |

## 6. Which Programme for Different Profiles

| Profile | Recommended Programme | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Tech professional (15+ yrs, Solution Architect), wants Master's** | NUS MComp in AI (SoC) OR NTU MSAI | Both are rigorous, respected. NUS slightly cheaper; NTU MSAI has dedicated AI focus from day one. MCAAI is also viable — less theory, more applied. |
| **Non-tech professional, AI upskilling** | NUS-ISS Graduate Diploma (applied) | More accessible entry requirements, less theoretical, practical industry projects. |
| **Quick upskilling (not Master's)** | Graduate Certificate (either university) | 4 courses, 6–12 months, stackable toward master's later. Minimal commitment. |
| **Manager/executive, AI literacy** | NUS Business School / NTU Exec Ed short courses | No exams or capstone projects; strategy-focused, peer learning. 2–5 days. |
| **Career switch into AI** | NUS-ISS MTech AIS (SCTP eligible) OR NTU SCTP programme | Full subsidy via SCTP + training allowance. Industry project component. Lower entry barrier. |
| **Research-oriented / PhD aspiration** | NUS SoC MComp in AI | Stronger AI research group, deeper theoretical foundations, dissertation option. |
| **Cross-disciplinary (e.g., Finance + AI)** | NTU MCAAI (new) or NUS-ISS | Designed for non-CS backgrounds. MCAAI explicitly targets diverse disciplines. |

### Detailed Assessment for Jack Liu Shurui

**Profile:** Solution Architect at Crédit Agricole CIB, 15+ years in tech, Singapore-based.

| Programme | Fit Assessment |
|---|---|
| **NUS MComp in AI** | ⭐ Strong fit. Deep technical curriculum, well-regarded degree, part-time option. Fees ~SGD 40-50K subsidised. Requires computing background — Jack has this. |
| **NTU MSAI** | ⭐ Strong fit. Dedicated AI master's, rigorous curriculum. Slightly more expensive (~SGD 45-50K). Theory-heavy. |

**Recommendation:** If the goal is a master's degree, NUS MComp in AI offers the best combination of cost (~SGD 40-50K subsidised), reputation, and flexibility. NTU MSAI is a strong alternative with a dedicated AI focus. For maximum affordability, NUS-ISS MTech AIS via SCTP (potentially fully subsidised) is worth exploring.

### Graduate Certificates & Short Courses

- **Graduate Certificates (NUS):** Simpler application, often rolling intake. Apply directly via NUS SCALE or NUS-ISS.
- **Graduate Certificates / FlexiMasters (NTU):** Apply via NTU PaCE portal. Rolling intake for most courses.
- **SkillsFuture short courses:** Apply directly; intake varies (some have monthly starts).
- **Executive Education:** Register online, cohort-based, typically start every 1–3 months.

### Estimated Timeline for Full Master's (Part-Time)

```
Year 1: Apply and start Graduate Certificate (if stacking)
     ↓
Year 1–2: Complete Graduate Certificate(s), decide on Master's
     ↓
Year 2: Apply and matriculate into Master's programme
     ↓
Year 2–4: Complete Master's coursework + capstone/dissertation
     ↓
Year 4: Graduate
```

**Shortest path (direct Master's entry):** Apply immediately to MComp/MSAI. Complete in 2–3 years part-time.
**Safest path (stacking):** Start with a Graduate Certificate. Convert to Master's later if desired. No penalty for stopping early.
