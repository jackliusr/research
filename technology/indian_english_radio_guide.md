# A Comprehensive Guide to Indian Radio Stations Broadcasting in English

> **Last updated:** July 2026  
> **Intended audience:** English-speaking listeners in India and abroad seeking English-language radio content from India

---

## 1. The Indian Radio Landscape – An Overview

India has one of the world's largest and most vibrant radio markets, blending public broadcasting, private commercial networks, and a rapidly growing digital audio sector. While most radio content is in Hindi and regional languages (Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, etc.), English-language programming occupies a distinct and enduring niche — particularly in metropolitan areas and among cosmopolitan audiences.

### Key Players

- **All India Radio (AIR) / Akashvani** — India's state-owned public broadcaster (established 1936, under Prasar Bharati / Ministry of Information and Broadcasting). AIR runs over 400 stations nationwide, including dedicated FM channels with English content.
- **Private FM Networks** — Liberalised in the early 2000s. Major networks include **Radio Mirchi** (Times Group / ENIL), **Red FM** (Sun Group), **Radio City** (Music Broadcast Limited), **Fever FM** (HT Media), and **Radio One** (HT Media / Next Mediaworks).
- **Online / Streaming-Only Radio** — Platforms like JioSaavn, Gaana, and Wynk offer curated English stations. Pure-play internet radio and YouTube simulcasts are also growing.

### Regulatory Context

FM radio operates under licensing by the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting. Private FM stations face content-code restrictions on news/current affairs (recently relaxed). Foreign ownership is capped at 26%, and stations must air minimum Indian content quotas. Internet radio and streaming platforms operate under separate IT Act provisions with fewer restrictions.

## 2. All India Radio (AIR) / Akashvani – English-Language Services

AIR remains the single most important source of English-language radio in India, offering English across multiple channels and services.

### AIR FM Rainbow

AIR's urban FM channel with substantial English music and presenter content across 25 centres:

| City | Frequency | English Content Level |
|---|---|---|
| Delhi | 102.6 FM | High — music blocks, hourly English news, talk shows |
| Mumbai | 102.6 FM | High — English breakfast shows, international music |
| Kolkata | 107.0 FM | High — English programming, news |
| Chennai | 101.4 FM | Moderate — English music and news, bilingual (Tamil + English) |
| Bengaluru | 101.3 FM | High — strong English music rotation, youth-oriented RJs |

Other FM Rainbow cities with English content: Lucknow, Panaji, Kochi, Hyderabad, Chandigarh, Kanpur, Patna, Ranchi, and more. Hourly English news bulletins run on all Rainbow stations. Most RJs switch fluidly between English and Hindi/regional languages.

### AIR News – English Bulletins

- **AIR News 24x7** — Dedicated all-news channel (FM in select cities + AIR Live app)
- **Hourly English News** on all AIR networks (FM Rainbow, Vividh Bharati, etc.)
- **Flagship bulletins:** 06:00, 07:00, 08:00, 09:00, 13:00, 18:00, 21:00, 22:00 IST
- **AIR News Mobile App** — Android/iOS, live news stream + on-demand bulletins

### AIR External Services (External Services Division / ESD)

Broadcasts in 27 languages including English for audiences outside India, primarily via shortwave and digital:

- **General Overseas Service (GOS)** — Daily English broadcasts for Africa, Europe, the Middle East, South Asia, and East Asia covering news, current affairs, Indian culture, and music.
- Shortwave frequencies vary by region/time; schedules on the AIR website.
- Also simulcast via the AIR Live app and website.

### AIR Gold and Digital Streaming

- **AIR Live App** — Official mobile app (iOS/Android) streaming all AIR channels + podcasts
- **Prasar Bharati Website** (prasarbharati.gov.in) — Live streams of FM Rainbow, AIR News, FM Gold, Vividh Bharati
- **Global access:** No VPN required — streams freely worldwide

## 3. Major Private FM Stations with English Content

While most private FM stations are primarily Hindi/regional, many have carved out English-language segments for urban audiences.

### Radio Mirchi (98.3 FM / varies by city)

- **Parent:** Times Group (ENIL) | **Coverage:** 63+ cities (India's largest private FM network)
- **Primary language:** Hindi / regional; **English content:** select shows and segments
- **Notable English-friendly programming:**
  - **Mirchi @ Work** (Mumbai) — Flagship morning show with significant English content, celebrity interviews
  - **Love Guru with Kannan Gill** — Relationship advice show with English-heavy hosting (syndicated)
  - **English Retro Shows** — Select stations (Bengaluru, Delhi) run retro English music on weekends
  - **Mirchi Mix** — Select stations blend Hindi and English pop during drive-time
- **Access:** FM in most Indian cities; streaming at mirchi.in, TuneIn, JioSaavn, Mirchi app

### Red FM (93.5 FM / varies by city)

- **Parent:** Sun Group (Kal Radio) | **Coverage:** 70+ cities
- **Primary language:** Hindi / regional; **English content:** notable in metro stations
- **Notable English-friendly programming:**
  - **Red FM English Hour** — Dedicated English music hour in select cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru)
  - **Morning No.1 (Mumbai)** — English-heavy morning show with news, banter, international music
  - **International Top 40** — Weekly countdown of global chart hits
  - **Red FM Retro** — Weekend throwback English classics
- **Access:** FM in major cities; streaming at redfm.in, TuneIn, RadioGarden

### Radio City (91.1 FM / 104 FM / varies)

- **Parent:** Music Broadcast Limited (Jagran Prakashan) | **Coverage:** 39+ cities
- **Primary language:** Hindi / regional; **English content:** growing digital presence
- **Notable English-friendly programming:**
  - **City of Dreams** — Morning show with English content in metro stations
  - **International Music Showcase** — On Radio City Freedom (digital channel), featuring indie and international artists
  - **Radio City Freedom** — Digital-only channel with significant English indie/alternative music (Radio City app)
  - **Love Guru** — Relationship advice show (English-heavy)
- **Access:** FM in cities; streaming at radiocity.in, Radio City app, TuneIn

### Fever FM (104 FM / varies)

- **Parent:** HT Media | **Coverage:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Kolkata (Chennai service discontinued Dec 2025)
- **Primary language:** Hindi; **English content:** limited time blocks
- **Notable:** Fever Tonight (evening show with English segments), weekend English international music programs. Overall, Fever FM is overwhelmingly Hindi — English is limited.
- **Access:** FM in select cities; streaming at feverfm.in, TuneIn

### Radio One (94.3 FM)

- **Parent:** Next Mediaworks (HT Media) | **Coverage:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad (95.0 FM), Hyderabad
- **Primary language:** **English** — the only major private FM network in India broadcasting primarily in English ("international format").
- **Notable programming:** The Morning Show (breakfast English talk + music), drive-time English talk shows, retro English (classic rock, 80s/90s), international chart music, lifestyle and tech discussions in English.
- **Why it matters:** The go-to choice for predominantly English radio on FM in India; targets upscale, sophisticated audiences.
- **Access:** FM (94.3 FM most cities, 95.0 FM Ahmedabad); streaming at radioone.in, TuneIn, RadioGarden
- **Note:** Chennai licence surrendered October 2025 — service discontinued there.

### 91.9 FM (Various Brands)

Indigo 91.9 operated in select cities (Bengaluru, Chennai) with English/international music programming, but availability has fluctuated due to licence renewals and ownership changes. Check local listings.

## 4. Internet-Only and Digital Radio Stations

### Radio Hays
UK-based station with a significant Indian following. Programs Bollywood, Indian pop, and English music fusion content popular with diaspora and domestic listeners. **Access:** TuneIn, Radio Hays website.

### DESIblitz Radio
UK-based South Asian diaspora station with Indian music (Hindi, Punjabi, regional) mixed with English pop and English-language presenter shows. **Access:** TuneIn, DESIblitz website.

### Gaana Radio
Times Group streaming app offering curated English stations: English Top 40, English Indie, English Rock/Pop/EDM, and artist-based stations (Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran, etc.). Algorithm-driven with occasional presenter content. **Access:** Gaana app (iOS/Android), web.

### JioSaavn Radio
Reliance (Jio) platform with dedicated English Radio channel (live DJ content), International Hits, English Retro, Love Notes (English), Chill English, and mood/artist-based stations. **Access:** JioSaavn app (iOS/Android), web; bundled with Jio mobile plans.

### Wynk Radio (Airtel)
Airtel's streaming platform offering English Pop, Rock, 90s, Workout, and genre-based stations. Mostly algorithm-driven. **Access:** Wynk Music app; bundled with Airtel plans.

### YouTube Simulcasts
Many Indian stations simulcast on YouTube — search "Radio One Mumbai live," "AIR FM Rainbow live," "Mirchi live." No app install needed; works globally in any browser.

## 5. English News Radio

### BBC World Service
Widely available on FM in major Indian cities through AIR relays and local station agreements. Content: global news, current affairs, HARDtalk, Business Daily, The Newsroom, Outlook. **Access:** BBC Sounds app, TuneIn, FM (check local frequencies).

### NPR (National Public Radio, US)
Online-only in India. Content: Morning Edition, All Things Considered, science and culture. Popular among Indian professionals and diaspora. **Access:** NPR app, TuneIn, npr.org.

### AIR News
FM (select cities) + dedicated app. Indian and international news in English, government announcements, parliamentary coverage. Hourly bulletins on all AIR networks.

## 6. How to Access Indian Radio from Abroad

### TuneIn (Best General Option)
Free streaming of most major Indian English stations — Radio One, AIR FM Rainbow, AIR News, BBC World Service. Search for "Indian English," "India English music," or specific station names. **Platforms:** Web, iOS, Android, smart speakers, Alexa. Few geo-restrictions.

### Radio Garden
Free, no sign-up. Clickable world map: navigate to India → tap any green dot (active station). Best for discovery — explore cities with strong English content (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru). **Platforms:** Web, iOS, Android.

### All India Radio (AIR Live)
prasarbharati.gov.in and AIR Live app (iOS/Android). Access FM Rainbow, AIR News, FM Gold, External Services. No VPN required — streams globally. Also offers podcasts and on-demand English bulletins.

### Individual Station Websites/Apps
- **Radio One:** radioone.in (live 94.3 FM stream, accessible globally)
- **Radio Mirchi:** mirchi.in (select city streams)
- **Red FM:** redfm.in
- **Radio City:** radiocity.in (includes Radio City Freedom digital English channel)
- Note: Some stations block streaming outside India due to licensing — fall back to TuneIn or Radio Garden.

### Internet Radio Directories
- **radioindia.org** — Indian FM directory with streaming links
- **onlineradiofm.in** — 1000+ Indian stations; filter by language (English) and genre
- **radioindia.in** — Major station directory with live streaming

## 8. Recommendations by Listening Taste

### 🎵 International Pop / Rock
- **Radio One (94.3 FM)** — The best option; primarily English international format with heavy pop/rock rotation
- **AIR FM Rainbow** — Solid English music mix in Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru
- **JioSaavn English Radio** — Reliable for current chart hits

### 📻 English News & Current Affairs
- **AIR News** (App / FM) — Comprehensive Indian news in English
- **BBC World Service** — Global perspective, FM in Indian cities
- **NPR** (Online only) — US/international news, popular with professionals
- **AIR External Services / GOS** — English news for international audiences

### 🎙️ Indian English Talk Shows
- **Radio One Morning Show** (94.3 FM) — Best English talk + music mix in metro cities
- **Radio Mirchi — Love Guru** — Relationship talk in English (Kannan Gill)
- **Red FM — Morning No.1** (Mumbai) — English banter and topical discussion
- **Radio One Drive-time Shows** — Lifestyle, tech, culture in English

### 🕰️ Retro English Music
- **AIR FM Rainbow Retro Shows** — Weekend English retro blocks
- **Radio City Freedom** (Digital) — English indie and retro mix
- **Radio One Retro** — Classic rock, 80s/90s English hits
- **Red FM Retro** — Weekend old-school English blocks

### 🎸 Indie / Alternative / Niche
- **Radio City Freedom** (Digital) — Best for indie and alternative English music
- **College Radio Stations** — Campus stations (IIT, BITS Pilani) often run English indie shows on TuneIn

### 🌍 For Indian Diaspora (Outside India)
